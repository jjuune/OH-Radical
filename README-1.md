# OH Radical Global Brand Platform

> Human First, Earth Always

A premium biotech platform focused on OH Radical-based environmental purification technology.

---

# Project Overview

OH Radical is a next-generation environmental biotechnology company specializing in OH Radical-based sterilization and purification technology.

This project aims to build a premium global web platform that:

- Establishes technological credibility
- Strengthens ESG-oriented brand positioning
- Expands B2G/B2B partnership opportunities
- Supports future global market expansion
- Converts visitors into qualified business leads

The platform is designed not simply as a corporate website, but as:

- A Global Brand Experience Platform
- An Industrial Solution Hub
- A Future-Oriented Sales & Trust Platform

---

Instead, the platform should communicate:

- ESG and sustainability values
- globally scalable technology infrastructure

# GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)

## Objective

Optimize the platform for AI-powered discovery systems such as:

- ChatGPT
- Gemini
- Claude
- Perplexity
- AI Search Engines

---

## GEO Strategy Principles

### Structured Knowledge Delivery

AI systems prefer:

- clear hierarchy
- concise explanation
- semantic consistency
- structured factual information

### Entity-Based Branding

Strengthen semantic associations between:

- OH Radical
- CNT Technology
- Active Sterilization
- ESG Infrastructure
- Chemical-Free Purification

### AI-Friendly Content Structure

Recommended:

- short explanatory sections
- comparison tables
- FAQ structure
- glossary support
- industry use cases

Avoid:

- vague marketing language
- overly decorative copywriting
- excessively long technical explanations
